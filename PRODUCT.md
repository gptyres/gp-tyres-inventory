# GP Tyres & Mags Inventory Tracker

## Product

GP Tyres & Mags Inventory Tracker is an internal retail operations app for tyre, wheel, supplier-stock, POS, quote, invoice and stock-control workflows.

## Users

Primary users are GP Tyres & Mags staff serving customers, checking stock, preparing quotes, processing sales and handling supplier availability. Admin users also manage sensitive controls, sync and reporting.

## Experience

The app should feel fast, dense and work-focused. The visual language is dark industrial with strong red action states, compact panels, high-contrast text and clear operational controls.

## Principles

- Make stock, price and order decisions easy to verify.
- Prefer native app screens over standalone tools.
- Keep supplier and customer-facing information clearly separated.
- Keep business calculations deterministic and testable.
- Preserve existing workflows unless a change is explicitly requested.

## Accessibility

Controls should remain keyboard accessible, readable in the dark theme, usable on touch devices and safe on narrow screens without horizontal scrolling.
